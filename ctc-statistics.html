Simarkism Origins

Inspiring Sand started posting YouTube comments under Cracking the Cryptic's videos with how many times they've said what in each video, back in 2020. Inspiring Sand has been able to parse them in each video and we have taken it one step further and programmed them all into one app which parses all his comments.

What are Simarkisms?

Simarkisms are popular phrases that Simon and Mark say throughout their videos. Some of the popular ones are "bobbins", "you rotten thing", "knowledge bomb" and more. Since most of the viewers are from America, I believe most of them are because we (myself included) love the colloquialisms they use because they live in England.
